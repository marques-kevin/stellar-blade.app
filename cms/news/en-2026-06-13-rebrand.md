We've relaunched the music app for Stellar Blade. All 142 tracks from the official soundtrack are now available, organized by game locations like Eidos 7, Xion, the Wasteland, and more.

Browse location-based playlists, boss battle themes, vocal tracks, and story credits. You can also vote for your favorite characters in the character ladder.

Enjoy the music of Stellar Blade!
